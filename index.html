Violent Crimes vs. Arrests in the Five Most Populated States

By: Lindsey Spatola
Calirfonia, Texas, Florida, New York and Illinois are the largest states in the United States by population. California has the most arrests in 2015, so it would be safe to assuse it had the most violent crimes in each area. Thats is not the case. Texas has more violent crimes in cities outside metropolitan and nonmetropolitan counties than California. Violent crimes include the offenses of murder, rape (revised definition), robbery, and aggravated assault.
